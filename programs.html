Programs

We understand your employees are the only resource that increases in value as time passes. With that in mind, we offer a complete set of topics for your employee's needs.

ABC's of Leadership

This powerful program is designed to move your experienced and new supervisors to achieve higher performance levels by providing the skills needed to manage, coach, inspire and lead people, time, projects and tasks successfully and recognize when to use each technique to achieve peak perfomance.

Today's supervisors must be adaptive to communicate corporate directive, coach subordinates, give feedback that will give staff the encouragement and self-confidence to change.

Book now

Length:

Two days

Key Points:

  • Overcome early problems of leadership
  • Create goals and achieve them with a plan
  • Build and reinforce confidence in the team
  • Understand personality behavior as related to work behavior
  • Keep top performers at their most productive level
  • Encourage staff to adapt to - as well as initiate - change in the workplace

Assertive Communication

Participants will learn the secrets of successful communication, building personal credibility, delivering and receiving positive and negative feedback, improving listening skills, rephrasing for better understanding, dealing with difficult people, handling negative situations, setting boundaries and more.

The exercises are interactive, allowing participants to practice what they have learned in a fun and energetic environment. This is the ideal program for those needing to improve their basic communication skills or for those looking for ways to increase their credibility that leads to having an extra business edge.

Book now

Length:

One day

Key Points:

  • Explore the four basic behavioral styles and how to adapt to each
  • Utilize personal style for more effective communication
  • Understand the impact of body language and voice tones
  • Show the effective use of office communication tools
  • Share alternate strategies for dealing with difficult behaviors
  • Explain the difference between constructive feedback and criticism

"Time Out!" Stress Management

The key to understanding stress is realizing stress is in our minds. It is simply the way we percieve events and situations. The same situation can cause different stress reactions in different people.

The "Time Out!" program builds on the positives you are already doing while offering options to the negative things we do now.

It is estimated that stress costs American businessess 300 billion dollars annually due to poor productivity, conflict mistakes, days off work and increased medical costs.

Book now

Length:

Half day

Key Points:

  • Learn where stress comes from
  • Learn the symptoms of stress
  • Learn options to reduce stress
  • Construct a personal action plan to manage stress
  • Understand what is causing stress at this moment in life

Participants will learn how to make customer encounters more productive, positive and profitable by learning the skills to cope with client's demands including the difficult personalities.
